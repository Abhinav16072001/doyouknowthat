How To Make Lemonade At Home

Lemonade is a popular drink, and for good reason. It's the perfect balance of sweet and tart, making it extremely delightful on a hot day. It's also very easy to make at home, requiring only some basic ingredients and a few minutes of your time.

Lemonade recipe with step-by-step instructions and photos so that anyone can make it easily at home with only a few ingredients and some time. Here's are the step-by-step instructions -

Ingredients

  • 1   cup white, granulated sugar (can reduce to 3/4 cup)
  • 1   cup water (for the simple syrup)
  • 1/4   cup lemon juice
  • 2  to  3 cups cold water (to dilute)

  1. Make simple syrup

    Place the sugar and water in a small container and bring to a simmer. Stir so that the sugar dissolves fully and remove from heat.

    2. Related: Cold Coffee: How to make a Instant Cold Coffee

  2. Juice The Lemons

    While the water is heating for the syrup, juice your lemons. Depending on the size of the lemons, 4 to 6 of them should be enough for one cup of juice.

  3. Combine Lemon Juice, Syrup, and Water

    Pour the juice and the syrup sugar water into a serving jug. Add 2 to 3 cups of cold water and taste. Add more water if you would like it to be more diluted (though note that when you add ice, it will melt and naturally dilute the lemonade). If the lemonade is a little sweet for your taste, add a little extra juice to it.

  4. Chill

    Refrigerate 30 to 40 minutes. Serve with ice, sliced lemons, and your icy cold drink at home.
