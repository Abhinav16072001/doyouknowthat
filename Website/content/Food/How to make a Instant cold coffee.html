How to make a Instant Cold Coffee

Are you someone, who is madly in love with cold coffee but doesn't know how to make it at home? Well, we are here to help you out in making a Cafe-style Cold Coffee that you can enjoy with ice cream and chocolate syrup. Here's how you can make this cold coffee recipe easily at home. Here's are the following steps!

If you looking for the best cold coffee recipe then you have reached the best place for making the best icy cold coffee with step-by-step instructions. Here is an easy cold coffee recipe you can try to make cafe-style cold coffee at home! With its creamy and refreshing taste, cold coffee has enticed many people. The best part about this instant cafe-style cold coffee recipe is that you just need a few simple ingredients and you are all set to enjoy your summer noons!

Ingredients

  • 2   cup milk
  • 2   1/2 teaspoon coffee
  • 2   tablespoon fresh cream
  • 1   teaspoon honey
  • 2   tablespoon vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2   cup Ice cubes

  1. Blend in the ingredients

    Put ice cubes in a blender jar and add coffee, milk, honey, cream, and about two tablespoons of vanilla ice cream. If you donâ€™t have a blender just take a shaker and put milk, honey, coffee, cream and give it a nice stir and then shake it! If you like your cold coffee extra creamy, then add some fresh cream and shake it well.

  2. Add ice cream and chocolate syrup to relish!

    Mix it in the mixer, take some ice cubes and then pour it into glasses. Put a scoop of vanilla ice cream and top it with some chocolate syrup, nuts, and raisins. Enjoy this recipe with some delectable pleasure!
