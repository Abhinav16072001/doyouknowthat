How To Make Chocolate Shake At Home

Nothing beats a creamy, thick chocolate shake alongside a nice burger and fries. But you don't have to go to a fast-food joint to get a great milkshake. With only three ingredients (milk, ice cream, and chocolate syrup) our chocolate milkshake is an excellent treat, coated with a nice dollop of whipped cream.

Whole milk will give the richest taste; a quality full-fat ice cream made with a few simple ingredients (no additives or thickeners) will add silky texture, and good chocolate syrup will give your shake the perfect flavor. Use nice dark or milk chocolate for the shavings.

Ingredients

  • 2   cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2   cup whole milk, cold
  • 1/4   cup chocolate syrup
  • 1/4   cup chocolate chips, optional
  • Shaved chocolate, garnish
  • Whipped cream, garnish

  1. Assemble All The Ingredients And Blend It

    Gather the ingredients and place the ice cream, milk, and chocolate syrup into the blender. If using chocolate chips, add those as well. Be mindful that the harder the ice cream is, the better, as the blending process can liquefy the ice cream too much and make the milkshake too thin. Blend the ingredients until completely smooth.
