5 Best Shonen Anime Of All Time

  1. One Piece

    Eiichiro Oda’s saga of Monkey D. Luffy and his gang of pirates in search of ultimate treasure being the best shonen series of them all. It stands as the last remaining member of Shonen Jump’s Big 3 over twenty years after it began, and Oda just keeps surprising people with new reveals and callbacks to plotlines from years.

    Supposedly anime is around eighty percent done with the storyline now, but considering the length of the anime, if that’s an accurate number the remaining twenty percent is still going to take some time.

  2. NARUTO & NARUTO SHIPPUDEN

    Alongside Bleach and One Piece, Naruto would become part of Shonen Jump’s Big 3. While Bleach lost quite a bit of its luster by the end, Naruto was just as strong as ever when it finished.

    Naruto was so popular it even wound up inspiring a monthly manga series based on Naruto’s son, Boruto, adding a generational aspect to the series. The only thing holding this series back from even greater heights is the absurd amount of filler.

  3. FULLMETAL ALCHEMIST: BROTHERHOOD

    Edward and Alphonse Elric’s journey to recover their bodies after a mistaken attempt to bring their mother back to life using alchemy is definitely one of the most captivating stories in anime.

    That’s why it’s no surprise this anime is on the top of shonen animes of all time.

  4. Bleach

    Bleach is considered one of the "big three" in shonen anime alongside Naruto and One Piece. The main character, Ichigo Kurosaki, is a high school student who becomes a Soul Reaper with the job of protecting people from malevolent spirits known as Hollows.

  5. MY HERO ACADEMIA

    Welcome to the adventures of Class 1-A, full of the Best Boys and Girls that anime has to offer. The lead best boy is Izuku Midoriya, a young teenager who starts out without superpowers in a world where almost everyone has a Quirk. When Midoriya meets his idol and biggest superhero of them all, All Might, he winds up pulled into an adventure that will lead to him becoming the greatest hero ever.
