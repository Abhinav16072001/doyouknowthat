How to Make Easy Breakfast Sandwich

Paneer sandwich is a simple, easy, and tasty sandwich that can be made for breakfast, evening snacks, or for a light lunch or dinner. There are many ways of making paneer sandwiches. In this post, I am sharing a delicious recipe.

Paneer Sandwich recipe with step-by-step instructions and photos so that anyone can make it easily at home with only a few ingredients and some time. You can also enjoy a sandwich with cold drinks. Here's are the step-by-step instructions -

Ingredients

  • 1   cup paneer/cottage cheese (grated)
  • 2   tbsp carrot (grated)
  • 2   tbsp capsicum (chopped)
  • 2   tbsp corn (boiled)
  • 1   tbsp coriander (finely chopped)
  • 1/4   tsp kashmiri red chilli powder
  • 1/4   tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4   tsp salt
  • 2   tbsp tomato sauce
  • 4   slice bread (white / brown)
  • 2   tsp green chutney
  • 2   tsp butter

  1. Making Stuffing

    Firstly, in a large mixing bowl take 1 cup grated paneer.

  2. Adding Ingredients and Mixing

    Add 2 tbsp carrots, 2 tbsp capsicum, 2 tbsp boiled corn, and 1 tbsp coriander. then add additionally, add ¼ tsp chili powder, ¼ tsp cumin powder, ¼ tsp salt, and 2 tbsp tomato sauce. Lastly, mix well making sure all the spices are combined well.

  3. Start Making the Sandwich

    Now spread green chutney over a bread slice and then spread green chutney over a bread slice then cover again with bread slice spread with green chutney. At last grill golden or toast on Tawa by spreading butter.

  4. Serving

    Finally, cut half and serve the paneer sandwich recipe.
